<b>Speedy Launcher Fork</b> achieves a beautiful balance between performance and usability.

No doubt it is exceedingly minimal - no icons, no auto update applications list, nothing that could waste your battery.

Here's it's help text:

Tap on an <b>app</b> to launch it

Long press on an <b>app</b> to open its system settings.

Tap <b>▦</b> to show/hide app also without search text.

Long press <b>▦</b> to show/hide confirm dialog using shortcut.

Every other symbol is a shortcut that launches the app you want with just a tap or long press.

Launch "Speedy Launcher" from itself to reload the app list and view this help text again.
